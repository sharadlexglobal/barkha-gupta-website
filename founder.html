Professional Profile

Ms. Barkha Gupta
Retired District Judge, Delhi
Founder, Just People by Barkha Gupta

Ms. Barkha Gupta is a distinguished retired District Judge with over 32 years of exemplary service in the Delhi judiciary. Through Just People, she now channels her extensive judicial experience into empowering individuals and families navigating complex family and matrimonial law matters.

Career Highlights

  • District Judge (Commercial), Delhi Higher Judicial Service
  • Commercial Court Judge dealing with high-value disputes
  • Presiding Officer of Mahila Court (Women's Court)
  • Family Court Judge addressing matrimonial disputes

Mediation & ADR Expertise

Empaneled as mediator with:

  • Delhi District Mediation Centres (approved by Hon'ble High Court of Delhi)
  • Supreme Court Legal Services Committee
